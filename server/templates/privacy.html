Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 24-Nov-2024

ICECream Vision (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our browser plugin and related services (the “Service”).

1. Information We Collect

1.1 User Information

  • When you sign in using Google OAuth, we collect your email address.
  • This email is used for account creation, authentication, and service-related communication.

1.2 User-Generated Data

  • Screenshots: We store the screenshots you submit for processing through our Service.
  • Questions: We store the questions or prompts you submit along with the screenshots.
  • All screenshots and questions are stored securely for 12 months to allow auditing, service improvements, and training purposes.

1.3 Payment Information

  • We do not collect or store any payment-related information, such as credit card or bank details.
  • Payments are processed securely through trusted third-party payment gateways.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide the Service: Process screenshots and questions submitted to third-party AI services and return results.
  • To Maintain the Service: Audit and analyze stored data to improve and enhance the functionality of our Service.
  • To Communicate: Notify you of updates, respond to support requests, or send service-related communications.

3. Data Handling by the Plugin

3.1 Limited Browser Access

  • The browser plugin does not read, store, or access any data from your browser unless:
    • You trigger an action: By clicking the plugin icon and initiating a request.
    • Accessing the current active tab: The plugin only accesses the content of the currently active browser tab where the user operates the plugin.

3.2 No History or Browsing Data

  • The plugin does not access or store your browsing history or other unrelated data from your browser.

4. Sharing Your Information

4.1 Third-Party AI Services

  • To process your requests, screenshots and questions are shared with third-party AI services such as OpenAI or Gemini.
  • These services analyze the content and return results, and their respective privacy policies apply to this data.

4.2 Trusted Payment Gateways

  • Payment details are handled by trusted payment gateways (e.g., Stripe, PayPal) and are never stored or processed by us.

4.3 Legal Compliance

  • We may share information if required to comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or enforceable government requests.

5. Data Storage and Retention

  • Retention Period: User data, including email addresses, screenshots, and submitted questions, is stored for 12 months and securely deleted thereafter.
  • Storage Security: We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect stored data from unauthorized access.

6. User Choices and Control

  • Access and Deletion: You can request access to your stored data or ask us to delete it by contacting [Insert Contact Information].
  • Consent Withdrawal: You may revoke access to your data or uninstall the plugin at any time.
  • Data Sharing: By using the Service, you consent to sharing your data with third-party AI services as described.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our Service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete it.

9. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
